What Are Semantic Elements?

Semantic elements clearly describe their meaning to both the browser and the developer.

Examples: <header>, <main>, <article>, <footer>

Examples of Semantic Elements

<article>

Used for blog posts, news articles, forum posts, or independent content.

<section>

Used to group related content within a page.

<aside>

This is an aside. Often used for: - Sidebars - Ads - Related links
